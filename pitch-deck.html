Why it's non-trivial
"Not a ChatGPT wrapper."
Claude doesn't retrieve — it synthesizes. It reads conflicting signals from three independent sources, resolves ambiguity (common names, transliterations), assesses confidence, and writes an auditable explanation.
Parallel data fetching — all three sources hit simultaneously, not sequentially. That's why it's 8 seconds, not 30.
1.4M sanctions entries across 200+ live-updated lists — OpenSanctions is the same data Refinitiv charges €50K/year to access.
Structured output enforced — every report has a machine-readable risk score, making downstream automation trivial.
